How Can Someone Join the Pro Tour

Joining the professional tour starts at the lower levels known as ATP Challenger Tour. This is the starting point to become a pro tennis player. Many of the world's best players went through this circuit and worked their way up to the top of the world ranking.

After getting enough points from the Challenger Circuit you can then play ATP tournaments. These come in levels and range from 250, 500, 1000, and Grand Slam. The Grand slams are the top tournaments of every year and there are four of them: Austrailian Open, French Open, Wimbledon, and US Open. Each grand slam is very prestigous and winning it can send you to the top of the rankings.
